Munhakdongne Publishing Group

Established in 1993

Munhakdongne Publishing Group started with publishing literature, and now we present a wide variety of publishing areas including general
non-fiction, art, self-help, pop science, life-style and comics. Munhakdongne Publishing Group currently consists of 30 imprints and subsidiaries,
publishing 400~500 new books every year.

Through Munhakdongne Novel Award and Munhakdongne New Author Award serving as a gateway for young authors, we have been discovering the
best writers in Korea including Kim Young-ha, Eun Hee-kyung, Cheon Myeong-kwan, Kim Un-su, Cho Nam-joo, and Chang Kang-myoung. By
awarding writers with Young Author Award and Kim Seung-ok Literary Award, we encourage writers on their work, while striving to present great
Korean literary works overseas.

Munhakdongne Publishing Group

  • No. 1 in sales among Korean publishers in 2017, 2019
  • General publishing group with 12 subsidiaries
  • A trend-setting publishing group with high-quality skills in management,
    editing, design, and marketing
  • A leading publisher in globalizing K-literature

Established in 1993 and showing constant growth for the last 28 years, Munhakdongne Publishing Group is
the best publisher in Korea. Munhakdongne scored first in sales among Korean publishers in 2017 and 2019.

Through Munhakdongne Novel Award and Munhakdongne New Author Award serving as a gateway for
young authors, we have been discovering the best writers in Korea including Kim Young-ha, Eun Hee-kyung,
Cheon Myeong-kwan, Kim Un-su, Cho Nam-joo, and Chang Kang-myoung. By awarding writers with Young
Author Award and Kim Seung-ok Literary Award, we encourage writers on their work, while striving to
present great Korean literary works overseas.

Munhakdongne Publishing Group, with 12 subsidiaries, has original plans and precise editing skills to make
good books that touch the readers’ heart, in every field including literature, history, philosophy, science,
economics, education, art, comics, and life-style.

Main Books and Authors

  • We have the best writers in Korea, aging from junior to senior, including Hwang Sok-yong, Kim Hoon, Han Kang, Shin Kyung-sook, Kim Young-ha, Song Sok-ze, Eun Hee-kyung, Kim Yeon-su, Kim Ae-ran, Cho Nam-joo, Kim Un-su, Kim Keum-hee, Chung Se-rang and Choi Eun-young.
  • We have former and current expert writers such as the trend expert Kim Ran-do, former presiding judge of Seoul Central District Court Moon You-suk, and the emergency medicine physician Namkoong Ihn.
  • We published main works of the masters of world literature such as Tolstoy, Dostoevskii, Hesse, Hemingway, and the renowned writers including Paulo Coelho and Haruki Murakami.
  • We published numerous books that won global literary awards such as the Nobel Prize in Literature, Man Booker Prize for Fiction, Pulitzer Prize, and Prix Goncourt.
  • We published many bestsellers in Humanities, including 『Capital in the Twenty-First Century』 『Capital and Ideology』, 『The Eight Words』, 『The Book Is the Axe』, 『Declaration of the Individualist』, 『Roman Law Lessons』, 『Amor Fati: For the Grownups who Just Took the First Step in the Real World』, and 『The Art of Happiness』.

Munhakdongne introduced and published major works of many authors: masters Hwang Sok-yong and Kim Hoon, Shin Kyung-sook and Han Kang, the winner of the Man Booker International Prize, senior writers who are leading Korean literature such as Kim Young-ha, Eun Hee-kyung, Song Sok-ze, Kim Yeon-su, and Kim Ae-ran, and young authors including Choi Eun-young, Chung Se-rang, Kim Keum-hee, the future leaders of Korean literature.
We published Munhakdongne Korean Literature Collection which shows great achievements of Korean literature; Munhakdongne World Classics Collection, a timeless fictional library; Munhakdongne Collection of Poems that aims for refreshing senses and deeper thoughts; and the Anthology of Award-winning Young Authors, an annual series continued for the past thirteen years. We also published numerous major works of writers who won international literary awards such as the Nobel Prize, Man Booker Prize for Fiction, Pulitzer Prize, and Prix Goncourt. Munhakdongne is constantly introducing the works of Nobel Prize winners, such as Hermann Hesse, Patrick Modiano, Toni Morrison, Mario Vargas Llosa, Mo Yan, Alice Munro, and Bob Dylan. Also, we publish main works of Paulo Coelho, known as an enchanting storyteller and the author of 『The Alchemist』, and Haruki Murakami, an internationally renowned author of 『1Q84』.
Munhakdongne also breathed in new life into works that were first published by another publisher: 『Midnight’s Children』 by Salman Rushdie; 『The Shadow of the Wind』 by Carlos Ruiz Zafón; 『The Theory of Light and Matter』 by Andrew Porter; 『The History of Love』 by Nicole Krauss; 『Tinkers』 by Paul Harding, and more.
We published books in Economics and Management: 『Capital in the Twenty-First Century』 and 『Capital and Ideology』 by a well-known economist Thomas Piketty, 『Insanely Simple』 and 『Think Simple』 by Ken Segall. We also published many works of outstanding writers in Humanities: Kim Ran-do, Park Woong-hyun, Moon You-suk, and Han Dong-il.

Publishing Network Overseas

  • A leader in introducing Korean literature
  • Exporting various books of Korean writers such as Shin Kyung-sook, Kim Un-su, Cho Nam-joo, Chung Se-rang, Choi Eun-young, Kim Ran-do, and Moon You-suk.
  • Exclusively working with an international scouting agency based in New York City.
  • Constant and stable transaction through local workshops and various book fairs such as the Frankfurt Book Fair

Munhakdongne plays a leading role in exporting not only Korean literature, but also books of various fields. Through a local scout in New York, we are directly introduced to foreign writers, and are introducing books of Korean authors. Since Munhakdongne took part in the Frankfurt Book Fair as a guest of honor in 2005, we are maintaining a stable network with foreign agencies and publishing companies through annual book fairs, fellowship programs, business trips, and copyrights workshops.

In the year 2020, more than 100 books of Munhakdongne Publishing Group were exported overseas. 『Tangerine Green』, a recent work of Cho Nam-joo, was sold to six countries after a heated auction, and 『The Plotters』 by Kim Un-su was sold to 26 countries. 『Shoko’s Smile』 by Choi Eun-young met readers of eight countries including the United States and England. Other works of female authors, including Chung Se-rang, Kang Hwa-gil, Baik Sou-linne, Lim Sol-ah, are actively being introduced. Books of various fields by authors such as Lee Byung-ryul, Kim Ran-do, Moon You-suk, Namkoong Ihn, Ha Jung-woo, and Yozoh have met foreign readers and are waiting for more.

  • Munhakdongne

    The Quarterly

    Launched in the winter of 1994, Munhakdongne The Quarterly has provided writers with an arena to present their latest works. Munhakdongne aims at fostering a conductive environment for literary writing and the promotion of dialogue among writers and critics. Most important movements of contemporary Korean literature have been conceived through this magazine. Also, many talented young writers debuted in Munhakdongne. The magazine has established itself as the trend-setter of the Korean literature scene.

  • Munhakdongne

    Mysteria

    Munhakdongne launched a new magazine in the summer of 2015. Mysteria (Mystery + Hysteria) is a bi-monthly magazine for mystery novel fanatics, published by Munhakdongne’s mystery brand ‘Elixir.’ Consisted of articles and columns related to mystery and thrillers, Mysteria provides readers with a variety of fun reads including short stories written by prominent writers such as Joyce Carol Oates, Donald E. Westlake, William Irish, Shirley Jackson, Honobu Yonezawa, and more.

  • Munhakdongne

    Anthology of Award-winning Young Authors(13 Volumes)

    Constantly making effort to keep in tune with new literature through many projects, Munhakdongne has brought together an award-winning anthology of young authors. This anthology includes works by Munhakdongne Young Author Award-winning writers under 10 years of their literary debut. Munhakdongne introduces works of promising and unique authors. Have a look into each gripping story of young Korean writers who will be forthcoming Korea’s literary masters.

Korean Literature Collection

Munhakdongne Korean Literature Collection was released in commemoration of the 20th Anniversary of Munhakdongne’s establishment. The main selection criterion for this collection was based on the book’s success of making Korean literature shine over the 20 years with Munhakdongne and its readers. This collection will continue to expand with a variety of genres and authors of all generations and it will bear the role of connecting the uniqueness of Korean literature and the universal characteristics of world literature. From this collection, Korea will find the past, the present, and the future of modern literature.

World Classics Collection

Beginning with Leo Tolstoy’s ANNA KARENINA to Herman Melville’s MOBY DICK, we have a selection of 210 books and more to go for our World Classics Collection. The series includes eternal classics such as George Orwell’s 1984, Goethe’s FAUST, and Albert Camus’s THE STRANGER, as well as masterpieces written by Nobel Prize-winning authors like Mo Yan’s THIRTEEN STEPS, Oe Kenzaburo’s THE BEAUTIFUL ANNABEL LEE WAS CHILLED AND KILLED, Elfriede Jelinek’s THE PIANO TEACHER, J.M.G. Le Clezio’s THE GOLDEN FISH, and Mario Vargas Llosa’s THE FEAST OF THE GOAT. We also have notable contemporary fiction in this collection, books that have been great inspirations to the world from various countries. With our modern editing skills and smart design senses, we will continue to add book by book, carefully selecting pieces, and building a collection that will be remembered for its richness and fine quality.
